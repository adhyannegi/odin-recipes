Garlic Bread Recipe!

Desciption

Garlic bread is a popular side dish that is made by spreading a mixture of butter, minced garlic, and herbs onto slices of bread, and then baking or grilling the bread until it is crispy and golden. The butter mixture can vary depending on the recipe, but it is often made by combining butter, garlic, parsley or basil, and grated Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread (such as french baguette, sourdough or Italian bread)
  • Unsalted butter
  • Minced garlic
  • Fresh parsley or basil (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cut the bread into thick slices, about 1/2 inch, leaving the bottom crust attached
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the butter, minced garlic, parsley or basil, salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the butter mixture generously on one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Place the bread slices, buttered side up, on a baking sheet
  6. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the bread is crispy and golden brown.
  7. Optional: Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of the bread and bake for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the garlic bread from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
