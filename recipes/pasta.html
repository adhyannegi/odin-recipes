Pasta Recipe!

Description

Pasta is a type of food made from wheat flour and water, and often eggs and other ingredients as well. There are many different types of pasta, each with their own unique shape and texture. Some popular types of pasta include spaghetti, linguine, fettuccine, penne, and macaroni. Pasta is typically cooked by boiling it in water for a few minutes, and it can be served with a wide variety of sauces, including marinara, alfredo, pesto, and meat sauce. Pasta is a staple food in Italian cuisine and widely popular around the world. It is versatile, easy to cook and can be paired with variety of ingredients to make an delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (such as spaghetti, linguine, fettuccine, penne, or macaroni)
  • Water
  • Salt for seasoning the pasta water

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and stir to prevent the pasta from sticking together.
  3. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, or until it is al dente (cooked but still firm to the bite).
  4. While pasta is cooking, you can prepare the sauce. This could be a simple olive oil and garlic sauce, or a more elaborate sauce made with tomato, cream, herbs, or meat.
  5. Reserve a cup of the pasta water before draining the pasta.
  6. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  7. Add the prepared sauce to the pasta, and toss to combine.
  8. If the sauce is too thick or the pasta is dry, add some of the reserved pasta water and toss again.
  9. You can also add grated cheese, fresh herbs, or other toppings if desired.
  10. Serve the pasta in bowls, and enjoy.
